Mimic Farms

- Northport, Michigan -

Mimic Farms is developing a perennial polyculture Agroforestry system in Northport, Michigan that is intented to imitate the Beech-Maple-Basswood plant community native to our region.

We are primarily growing Hybrid Hazelnuts and Chinese Chestnuts which are related to the Beech (Fagaceae) and Amerian Hop Hornbeam (Betulaceae) family of trees that thrive abundantley in the soil surrounding our property. Additionally we are testing a small planting of Paw Paw, to assess its growth and adaptability within our area.

Our focus is to use solutions that mimic nature on a hyper-local level to establish and ecologically resiliant system that helps promote soil health, increases biodiversity, and regeneratively produces high yields of nutrient dense food.
